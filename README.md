# TeenTitansCommunicator
This is a walkie talkie that is meant to look like the communicator from Teen Titans. Each of the walkie-talkies will have their own ID with an image, and when they are online, the other screens will show their ID. It will also beep when you start speaking.

I made this because I saw a pinterest post of a teen titans communicator made out of a bubble tape roll. And I realized that would be a really fun project, and I could give one to two of my friends. We talk about who's who from TV Shows a lot, and this is one of them, so I could give my friends each one personalized to who they were.
